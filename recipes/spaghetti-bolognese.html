Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a hearty Italian classic that combines tender spaghetti with a rich, flavorful meat sauce. To prepare, saut√© finely chopped onions, garlic, carrots, and celery in olive oil until soft, then brown ground beef or a mix of beef and pork. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and a mix of Italian herbs like oregano and thyme. Let the sauce simmer on low heat for at least an hour, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully. Toss freshly cooked spaghetti with the sauce, garnish with grated Parmesan and fresh basil, and serve warm for a comforting and delicious meal.

Ingredients

For the sauce:

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Tomato paste
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Beef stock
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried thyme or Italian seasoning
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt and black pepper

For the pasta

  • Spaghetti
  • Salt

For garnish

  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients
  2. Cook the sauce
  3. Cook the spaghetti
  4. Combine and serve
